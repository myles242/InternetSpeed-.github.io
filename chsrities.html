Charities and Organizations addressing internet Accessibility

Human-I-T

Human-I-T is a nonprofit organization that helps people get the equipment and internet access they need to be successful in today's digital world. They refurbish and distribute donated equipment, and also help people find affordable internet options in their area. To get started, all you need to do is fill out a form with your information and a volunteer will contact you to discuss your options. Link:Human-I-T

EveryoneOn

EveryoneOn is a nonprofit that helps low-income families access affordable internet service and devices. Its main program, Connect2Compete, partners with broadband providers to offer discounted plans to eligible families. Additionally, it provides free digital literacy training for adults. Link:EveryoneOn

Mobile Citizen

Mobile Citizen is a provider of low-cost wireless internet for nonprofit organizations, schools, and social welfare agencies. The service uses the Sprint network and offers a range of plans from free to $10 per month for eligible organizations. All plans include unlimited data. Link:Mobile Citizen
